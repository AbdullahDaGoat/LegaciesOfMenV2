Legacies of Men: A Movement for Men's Mental Wellness

Joe Truax, the founder of Legacies of Men, is a kind, socially awkward 40-year-old man fighting to keep men alive during this mental health crisis. Legacies of Men is a 100% forever-free, non-profit, non-religious, non-political COMPREHENSIVE men's mental wellness MOVEMENT, engineered to be the ONLY support system GOOD men from ALL walks of life will ever need.

Our Goals:

  • Prevent male suicide
  • Help men - and women by proxy - find and maintain non-toxic relationships
  • Unite communities worldwide

Our Meetings:

There are AT LEAST two meetings each week. One is called Legacies of Men: Unburdened, which helps you become aware of and dispose of things in your life that don't benefit you. The other is called Legacies of Men: Growth, which helps you become the kind of man the world will NEVER want to lose.

Our Slogan:

"Remembered For Good" means two things: remembered for the good you've done, and remembered permanently.

What We Offer:

Legacies of Men is an unrivaled support system that will make your shoulders a little less burdened, or hopefully a LOT less burdened. The meetings are easy to follow along with, free, lifesaving, life-changing, and it's all being done because we care.

Come to a meeting and meet others like yourselves. You come to one, you're probably gonna be at all the rest.
